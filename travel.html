Los Angeles:
The City of Los Angeles is considered to be the capital of the world when it comes to entertainment seeing as, it's the home of a multiethnic population, tourists and ideal weather. It's downtown "is the largest government center outside of Washington, D.C " and it's one of the only cities to have remaining unique architecture like a wooden lighthouse and the largest historical theater. There are about 88 incorporated cities in The City of Los Angeles. San Fransisco:
San Fransico is located on the North of California. It is in between the Pacific Ocean and San Fransisco Bay. It is on of the United States cosmopolitian cities. San Fransisco is characterized as a very elegant,handsome, cool, living area. San Fransisco is one the most affected cities by earthqkakes in the US or even in the world. It's the home of a lot of immigrants from all around the world. It opens it's arms to Americans, Europeans, Asians making it a very diverse comunity. Tourism is a major source of income for San Fransisco. It has a lot of museums and historical land marks like, bridges, Coit Tower, Chinatown, North Beach, etc. Down bellow is a picture of this wonderful city...

San Diego:
The City of San Diego is refered to as "America's Finest City," and it is the sixth state with the largest population. It's located on the coast of the Pacific Ocean in Southern California. It also attracts turists and residents with it's gorgeous climate conditions. It has over 70 miles of beaches and hosts home to a big variety of animals."San Diego is also a rich and diverse cultural mecca" with thousands of different individuals. It also has a big group of people that focuses on Fine Arts and Amature Sports Players. A lot of these people come to San Diego in search of oportunity and training. Santa Monica:
Santa Monica is a beach front city in western California. Throughout the years, it has become a very popular destination and a "must visit" for tourists. The Santa Monica pier is one of the biggest attractions including carnival rides, activities and food. It is consideres as one of the most cyclist friendly cities as it provides endless miles of biking lanes. It is also known to having a perfect weather with the exeption of few rainy days. Promanade st. is a very popular downtown street where a lot of people meet and go shoping. It is mostly closed on sundays for pedestrians.
